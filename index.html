How we met

Will and Sharon met in a movie theater in Kansas City. During a trip where Sharon was visiting some mutual friends that she and Will have, Will got invited to hang out with the group. After the movie the group started talking and Will noticed Sharon's love for traveling and Sharon didn't notice Will at all.

After some time, Will slid into Sharon's DMs and they started talking. They noticed lots of simlarities in one another. A love for travel, a heart for serving in their churches, a love for God. They also noticed something that neither one of them wanted to share with anyone ever.

Will and Sharon were both sinners. Will and Sharon weren't perfect. This made it harder to love one another, but in spite of their sin Jesus loved Sharon and Will. What Will and Sharon deserved for their sins was an eternal death, seperated from God. However Jesus came to die on the cross for the sins that Will and Sharon (and you!) had done. Jesus paid for those sins on the cross so that you wouldn't have to. Both Sharon and Will believed that Jesus had died for their sins. Because of Jesus's death Sharon and Will could have grace for each other and love one another. They loved because God first loved them.
